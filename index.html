Gary Domingo

Underground New York Stand-Up Comedian

A Myth in the Making
Gary Domingo built his reputation the hard way — late nights, sold-out basements, smoke-thick comedy rooms scattered across New York City. Known for his razor-dry delivery and unshakable stage presence, Gary became a quiet legend in the underground scene, performing sets at clubs where half the audience came just to see if the rumors were true.
The Six-Year Silence
In 2019, at the height of his momentum, Gary stepped away from touring completely. No explanation, no farewell show — just a sudden exit from the circuit. For six years, the city kept whispering his name. Comics swapped stories. Clubs kept a slot open “just in case.”
The Return
After six years offstage, Gary Domingo is stepping back into the lights. New projects. New material. A sharper edge than ever.
